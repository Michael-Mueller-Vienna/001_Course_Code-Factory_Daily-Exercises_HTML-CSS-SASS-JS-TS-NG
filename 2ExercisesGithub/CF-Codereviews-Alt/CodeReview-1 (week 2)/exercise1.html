The Best Ribeye

Ingredients and Tools

  • 500g Ribeye Steak
  • A ceramic pan
  • 3 garlic toes
  • A pinch of salt
  • A bit of pepper

Directions

  1. Put Ribeye steak 30 minutes before grilling out of the fridge.
  2. Pre-heat the ceramic pan to maximum temperature then sharply grill steack 3 minutes on each site use a timer.
  3. Wrap the steak into a tinfoil and put the garlic toes into the pan without gas the remaining heat grills the garlic.
  4. After 3 minutes take the steak oout of the tinfoil and pour the garlic gravy over the steak and add salt and pepper to your liking.
